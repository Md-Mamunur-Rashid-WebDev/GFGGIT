Juniper

Information Technology

Implement a robust quote-to-cash (QTC) tool to streamline subscription management, unify lead-to-cash processes, and provide comprehensive supply chain insights.

Project

Details

Scope of Work . Sales Cloud, Salesforce CPQ

Juniper Mist is dedicated to delivering network experiences that transform how people connect, work, and live in the dynamic 5G and cloud era. Juniper Mist’s solutions power essential connections across various sectors, from education to healthcare to secure banking, by challenging the inherent complexity of modern networking.

Obstacles

Juniper Mist faced several critical challenges in managing its subscription-based services and optimizing its lead-to-cash processes.

Subscription Management

The management of recurring subscription revenue was crucial for Juniper Mist's growth. They needed a tool that could efficiently handle the complexities of subscription management.

Diverse Go-to-Market Strategies

Juniper Mist employed various go-to-market strategies, each with its own unique lead-to-cash process. They sought a unified system to manage these diverse strategies effectively.

Supply Chain Visibility

To ensure efficient supply chain management, Juniper Mist required comprehensive insights and real-time visibility into their operations.

Solution Approaches

Juniper Mist engaged GFGG IT to address these challenges.

The following solution approaches were implemented:

Subscription Management with Salesforce CPQ

GFGG IT seamlessly integrated Salesforce CPQ to manage subscription-based services efficiently, facilitating future growth and building enduring customer relationships.

Unified Lead-to-Cash Process

Salesforce CPQ enabled Juniper Mist to manage all their products and diverse business models within a single system. This unified approach streamlined lead-to-cash processes, reducing complexity and increasing operational efficiency.

Supply Chain Insights

Salesforce provided executives with real-time visibility into their supply chain, empowering them to plan and manage it accurately.

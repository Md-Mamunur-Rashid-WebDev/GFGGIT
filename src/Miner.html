Project

Details

Scope of Work . Sales Cloud, Service Cloud

Transamerica Corporation is a prominent American holding company encompassing various life insurance companies and investment firms, primarily operating within the United States. Transamerica offers a range of financial services, including life and supplemental health insurance, investments, and retirement planning.

Obstacles

Miner encountered several significant challenges in their field service operations.

Manual Processes

Their field service processes were entirely manual, leading to inefficiencies, delays, and the potential for errors.

Siloed Processes

Billing, work orders, and dispatching were handled separately, resulting in disjointed operations and billing errors.

Lack of Visibility

Miner struggled with poor visibility into the operations of third-party technicians, making it challenging to manage and monitor their performance.

Automation and Scaling

To keep up with growing demands, Miner needed to automate and scale their operations while improving customer service and transparency.

Solution Approaches

Transamerica partnered with GFGG IT to address these challenges.

The following solution approaches were implemented:

Automated and Integrated Dispatch

GFGG IT introduced automated and integrated dispatch, notification, and billing systems, streamlining the entire field service process.

Mobile Solution

Field technicians were equipped with a mobile solution that provided step-by-step guidance for completing tasks, tracked check-in/check-out times, travel time, and facilitated electronic signatures.

Holistic View

Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud were utilized to provide a holistic view of all technicians, allowing Miner to track work orders, dispatches, and invoicing efficiently.

Intelligent Dispatch Engine

An intelligent dispatch engine was implemented, dispatching technicians based on their quality scores, ensuring the right technician for the job.
